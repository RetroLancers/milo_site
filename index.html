Bio:

Milo Beckman is a writer, educator, and author of the book Math Without Numbers, an illustrated tour of dimensions, infinity and more (Penguin, 2022). Born in Manhattan, Milo began taking math classes at Stuyvesant High School at age eight, was captain of the New York City Math Team by age thirteen, and started attending Harvard at age fifteen, but he thinks you don't need any of these qualifications to enjoy math. His diverse projects and independent research have been featured in the New York Times, Good Morning America, the Huffington Post, the Economist, and others. Milo worked for three tech companies, two banks, and a US Senator before retiring at age nineteen to work on his book and teach math in New York, China, and Brazil.
